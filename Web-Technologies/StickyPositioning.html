BRIEF NOTE ABOUT STICKY POSITIONING

Basically this is a mixture of relative and fixed position, allowing the placed object to behave as if it is positioned relatively until it is passed to a certain threshold point (say 10 pixels from the top of the viewport), after which it becomes stationary. This can be used for example to have the navigation bar scroll the page to a certain point, then stick to the top of the page.
